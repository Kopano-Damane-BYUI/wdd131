Site Name

Site Name: Goju-kai Karate Gym

Description: The name represents the Goju-kai style of Karate, which is known for balancing hard and soft techniques. This name was chosen because it reflects the core principles of discipline, respect, and self-improvement.

Optional Domain Availability: gojukarategym.com

Site Purpose

The website is designed for prospective and current members of the Goju-kai Karate Gym. It will provide essential information, including:

  • Introduction to the gym and its philosophy
  • Interactive class schedule with filters
  • Instructor profiles and booking options
  • Contact information and inquiries form

Scenarios

Here are some scenarios that might help guide the content of the website:

  • What types of classes do you offer, and what’s the schedule like?
  • How do I contact an instructor to book a private lesson?
  • What’s the philosophy behind Goju-kai Karate, and how can it improve my discipline and self-control?

Color Schema

The selected color schema will reflect the dynamic and disciplined nature of Goju-kai Karate:

  • Primary Color: Red – used in headers and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color: Black – used for body text and footer background.
  • Accent Color: White – used for text and highlights to create contrast.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Primary Font: 'Roboto' – used for body text, paragraphs, and form fields.
  • Secondary Font: 'Merriweather' – used for headings (H1, H2, H3).
  • Optional Font: 'Oswald' – for subheadings or special emphasis sections.

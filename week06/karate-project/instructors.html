Sensei Hiroshi Tanaka

With over 30 years of experience, Sensei Hiroshi began his journey in Okinawa under the tutelage of Grandmaster Fujita. He has competed internationally, bringing home multiple gold medals in Kata. His philosophy centers on inner balance, discipline, and pushing limits with grace.

×

Senpai Akira Sato

Senpai Akira is a fierce Kumite champion known for his explosive speed and footwork. A mentor to many, he believes in conditioning both mind and body. He’s passionate about cardio training as a foundation for defense and confidence.

×

Senpai Haruto Yamada

From a shy child to a confident fighter, Haruto’s transformation through karate is inspiring. He specializes in practical self-defense techniques and empowering young kids with respect and resilience.

×

Senpai Riko Matsuda

Riko is a Kata technician with a love for perfection in form. Her calm yet firm teaching style makes her a favorite among beginners. She mentors with patience and precision, building a strong foundation in her students.
